## Description

Adding [TradeClaw](https://github.com/naimkatiman/tradeclaw) to the **JavaScript** section of awesome-quant.

## What is TradeClaw?

TradeClaw is a self-hosted, open-source AI trading signal platform built with Next.js and Node.js. It provides:

- **Multi-indicator signals**: RSI, MACD, EMA (20/50/200), Bollinger Bands — multi-indicator confluence scoring
- **Backtesting engine**: Test strategies against historical OHLCV data with win rate, profit factor, and Sharpe ratio
- **Paper trading**: Simulated trading with P&L tracking
- **Screener**: Scan 15+ assets simultaneously for signals above confidence threshold
- **Telegram alerts**: Real-time buy/sell notifications
- **REST API**: Full OpenAPI 3.0 spec for signal retrieval
- **Self-hosted**: Docker Compose deploy in one command

## Why it belongs here

awesome-quant lists quantitative finance libraries and tools. TradeClaw is a production-ready JavaScript/Node.js trading system that quantitative developers can self-host, extend, and integrate with their own strategies.

- GitHub: https://github.com/naimkatiman/tradeclaw
- Demo: https://tradeclaw.win
- License: MIT
- Language: JavaScript/TypeScript (Next.js 15, Node.js)
