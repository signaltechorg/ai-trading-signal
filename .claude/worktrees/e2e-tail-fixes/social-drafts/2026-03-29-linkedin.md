I just open-sourced TradeClaw — a self-hosted trading signal platform I've been building.

It generates real BUY/SELL/HOLD signals for forex, crypto, and metals using technical analysis (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, EMA, Stochastic). No subscriptions, no cloud lock-in. MIT licensed.

Why I built it:

I was paying $60/month for a signal service that went dark during a volatile week. The signals were a black box — I had no idea how they were generated.

So I built my own. The entire signal engine is readable TypeScript. Deploy with one command: docker compose up.

What's included:
- 12+ instruments across forex, crypto, metals
- Backtesting with win rate, Sharpe ratio, drawdown
- Paper trading with virtual portfolio
- REST API (OpenAPI 3.0)
- Telegram/Discord alerts
- Mobile PWA

Tech: Next.js 15, TypeScript, TimescaleDB, Redis, Docker.

If you trade or build fintech tools, I'd love your feedback.
